About Me

I am a senior at North Central College double majoring in Finance and Organizational Communication, with a minor in Japanese. I studied abroad at Aoyama Gakuin University in Tokyo, Japan in Spring 2015 when I passed the Japanese Language Proficiency Test N2 Level. On campus, I am involved as a co-president and founding member of PRSSA Affiliate Board, a Public Relations student organization, the social media officer of International Club, a veteran orientation leader, and past member of the North Central College Forensics Team (competitive public speaking).

During my free time I love to explore healthy recipes with peanut butter and green tea powder, analyze hot global issues using communication theories, and dream about backpacking across 5 continents.
